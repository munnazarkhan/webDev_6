Our Story

Born from a love of
great food

Founded in 2022, Cravings started as a simple idea: everyone deserves access to amazing food, delivered fast and reliably. What began as a small startup in a single city has grown into a platform serving hundreds of thousands of happy customers across India.

We believe that food is more than sustenance — it's culture, community, and joy. That's why we work tirelessly to connect the best local restaurants with hungry customers, and to empower our riders with fair pay and flexible work.
