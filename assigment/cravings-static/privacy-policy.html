Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cravings collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services. By using Cravings, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

  • Account info and profile data
  • Order history and transaction records
  • Device and usage data
  • Location data (when permitted)

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To personalise your experience
  • To send relevant updates and promotions
  • To improve our platform and services
  • For legal compliance purposes

  • With partner restaurants and riders to fulfil orders
  • With payment processors for transactions
  • With analytics partners (anonymized only)
  • With law enforcement when legally required

  • Essential cookies for platform functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand user behaviour
  • You can opt out via your browser settings
  • Third-party services may also use cookies

  • Data is retained for the duration of your active account
  • Order history is kept for up to 5 years
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time
  • Analytics data is anonymized and retained indefinitely

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Data portability
  • Opt out of marketing communications
